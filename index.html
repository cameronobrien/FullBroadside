About Full Broadside

We'll be good...one day...

Nullsec Static

We have a nullsec static, which means we will always have a wormhole to nullsec, for easy access to PVP content everywhere around New Eden.

Experienced Small Gang PvPers

Our pilots are experienced in small-gang PvP and nano-gang warfare. We're willing to teach anyone, as long as you're willing to listen!

Easy K-Space Access

With our C6 Static, you can find a K-Space nearly daily, allowing you to restock as needed and keep the flow of ships coming!

You Scout, You Keep

Keep the loot for any possible target that you scout for us to kill! A great way to make money by enjoying PVP activities.

Welcoming Community

We're always ready to welcome new friends and corpmates to enjoy the game with!

Always at work!

We're always ready to search for new content, no matter where it takes us, around the clock, 24/7, 365 days a year!

How to Join Full Broadside

Create an account on our forums and follow the directions in the Applications subforum. You can also idle in our Discord to speak with corp members.
